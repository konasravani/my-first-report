Campus Compass helps students prepare for campus placements by providing skills roadmaps, company requirements, project ideas, and interview preparation tips.

Skills Roadmap

Project Ideas

Interview Tips

  • Research the company before the interview.
  • Practice common interview questions.
  • Dress professionally and arrive early.
  • Be confident and communicate clearly.
